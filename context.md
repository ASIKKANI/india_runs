## 🧠 Deep Dive: Track 01 — The Data & AI Challenge (Intelligent Candidate Discovery)

This is the track we are building for.

### The Problem
Traditional candidate filters rely heavily on surface-level keyword matching. These systems miss "hidden gems"—candidates whose true potential, intent, and subtle behavioral signals are lost in the noise. 

### The Objective
Develop a robust, workable **Proof of Concept (PoC)** for a predictive ranking engine that acts as an intelligent AI recruiter. It must do more than filter; it must intelligently rank candidates based on deep understanding.

Your system should be capable of:
1.  **Deep Job Understanding:** Interpreting complex, nuanced job descriptions.
2.  **Contextual Relevance:** Moving beyond keywords to understand semantic fit.
3.  **Signal Integration:** Leveraging all data including profile attributes, career metadata, and crucial activity/behavioral signals.
4.  **The Output:** Delivering a lightning-fast, highly accurate, and expertly ranked shortlist of the best-fit candidates.

*Note: There is no fixed architecture. Builders have total creative freedom to choose their technical approach.*

### Submission Requirements
To submit for Track 1, you must provide a complete package containing:
*   **The Code:** A well-organized GitHub repository with your complete implementation.
*   **The Blueprint:** Clear documentation or a `README` explaining your methodology, technical choices, and system architecture.
*   **The Results:** A ranked output file in a predefined format detailing your recommended candidate shortlist (based on a structured challenge dataset provided by the organizers).

## 💡 Track 02 — Ideathon (Creating the Future of AI)

This track is focused on ideation and product strategy.

### The Challenges
Participants can choose one of three problem statements:
1.  **Challenge 1: The AI Systems Architect (Reimagining Work):** Design a groundbreaking technical AI system (self-running agents, super-smart search, complex AI coordination, or intelligent co-pilots) that improves how work gets done. *(For Developers, Engineers, System Architects).*
2.  **Challenge 2: The AI Growth Strategist (Making AI Go Viral):** Create a business strategy and product design to make a revolutionary AI platform explode in the market. Focus on user trust, monetization models, and viral sharing mechanics. *(For MBAs, Product Managers, Marketers).*
3.  **Challenge 3: The Everyday AI Innovator (Life, Made Better):** Design a simple, AI-powered feature or experience that enriches the daily rhythm of life in India. *(For Students, Creators, First-Timers).*

### Submission Requirements
*   **The Pitch Deck:** Your organized and compelling story submitted as a PDF.
*   **Target Statement:** Explicitly state which of the 3 problem statements you are tackling.
*   **The Core Solution:** A detailed breakdown of your AI-native idea (what it is and how it works).
*   **The User Journey:** A step-by-step map showing exactly how a user interacts with your solution.
*   **Visuals (Optional):** Mockups, wireframes, or other visuals illustrating the solution.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

*   **What is Redrob?**  
    Redrob is India's AI Operating System—the first AI super app built ground-up for the way India works, hires, learns, and earns (features include chat, resume building, job matching across 15M+ live jobs, interview prep, hiring intelligence, and an AI app store).
*   **Is there a registration fee?**  
    No, India Runs is completely free to enter.
*   **Can I participate as a team or solo?**  
    For **Track 1** (Data & AI Challenge) and **Track 2** (Ideathon), you can participate solo or in teams of **up to 4 members**.  
    For **Track 3** (Social Media Challenge), participation is **strictly individual**.
*   **I have never participated in a hackathon before. Is this for me?**  
    Yes. Track 2, Problem Statement 3 is specifically designed for first-time participants with no technical background.
*   **Who are the judges?**  
    Redrob's leadership team, AI researchers, founders of top Indian product companies, and senior engineers from Redrob's partner ecosystem.
*   **Can I participate in multiple tracks?**  
    Yes. Any registered INDIA RUNS participant can participate in all three tracks.
*   **What language can I submit in?**  
    English is preferred for written submissions, but Hinglish explanations, Hindi methodology notes, and regional language contexts are welcome where they add clarity.
*   **Will my submission or idea be used by Redrob?**  
    Your submission may be used by Redrob based on the judges' evaluation, with full credit given to you wherever it appears. The strongest submissions may be considered for collaboration or integration into Redrob's roadmap.
